About us

We are devoted to societal evolution through
premium quality education, high value development
and interpersonal skills.
At CrestHive International School, the intellectual,
physical, mental, social and spiritual development of
the child is our main focus. Here children are
groomed and guided to positively affect their world.
The children are surrounded by a caring, hardworking,
supportive and attentive instructional team.
