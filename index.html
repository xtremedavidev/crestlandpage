What we are

At CrestHive International School, the intellectual, physical, mental,
social and spiritual development of the child is our main focus.
Here children are groomed and guided to positively affect their
world. The children are surrounded by a caring, hardworking,
supportive and attentive instructional team.

Empowering Individualized Learning

We emphasize the process of learning rather than the product. We
know as a fact that children learn differently and through various
means, so we let them learn at their own pace.

We create a secure, warm, happy citadel for children to grow,
learn and mature into becoming notable leaders. The teachers are
educated, skillful, and motivated to familiarize themselves with
best practices in early child, primary and secondary education.
